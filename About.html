Our Story

Established in 2012, Cellven Life Sciences began as a modest franchising company in Karachi, Pakistan. Over time, the company developed into a well-recognized franchising organization focused on high-quality nutraceutical formulations.

The product portfolio covers multiple therapeutic areas, including Cardiology, Neurology, Urology, Gastroenterology, Dermatology, Gynecology, and General Health. With a regional office in Karachi, this steady growth reflects the trust placed in the company by healthcare professionals. Today, Cellven offers more than twelve formulations in various dosage forms such as sachets, tablets, drops, syrups, and caplets, designed to responsibly support the evolving needs of the healthcare industry.
